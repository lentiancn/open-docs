# HeyGen Introduction

> HeyGen is an AI-powered video generation platform

---

## Overview

HeyGen is a platform for creating AI-generated videos, allowing users to produce professional-quality video content using virtual avatars and text-to-speech technology.

---

## Key Features

- **AI Avatars**: Virtual human presenters
- **Text-to-Speech**: Convert text to natural voiceovers
- **Video Templates**: Pre-designed video formats
- **Custom Avatars**: Create personalized avatars
- **Multi-language Support**: Support for 100+ languages
- **API Access**: Programmatic video generation

---

## Use Cases

- Marketing videos
- Training content
- Product demonstrations
- Social media content
- Personalized messages
