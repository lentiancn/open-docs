# HeyGen Installation Guide

## Account Registration and Login

### Accessing the Official Website

Visit the HeyGen official website at https://heygen.com and click the "Get Started For Free" or "Sign Up" button to begin creating an account. You can register using your email address or sign in quickly with a Google account. The registration process is simple - just fill in basic information and verify your email to complete.

After first login, the system will guide you through basic setup, including selecting your language and familiarizing yourself with core features. New users are encouraged to complete the onboarding tutorial to understand the platform's basic operations, laying a foundation for subsequent video creation.

### Account Type Selection

HeyGen offers four main account types: Free, Creator, Pro, and Business. The free version allows 3 videos per month, up to 3 minutes each, suitable for initially exploring the platform's capabilities. The Creator plan at $29/month offers unlimited video creation with more advanced features. The Pro plan at $99/month provides 10× more premium feature access and 4K video export. The Business plan at $149/month is suitable for team collaboration, with additional seats at $20/month.

## Interface Navigation

### Main Interface Overview

After logging in, you'll see HeyGen's main interface. The left navigation menu includes major functional modules like Create Video, Avatar Management, Scene Selection, Project Management, and Settings. The central area is the content display zone, showing your video project list and template recommendations. The right panel provides quick setting options including voice selection, language settings, and preview functions.

The top navigation bar contains account information, usage quota display, and help access. The bottom status bar shows current task progress and system notifications. After familiarizing yourself with the interface layout, you can quickly navigate to required functions, improving work efficiency.

### Feature Modules Introduction

Create Video is the core function - click to enter the video creation workspace. Avatar Management allows you to browse, create, and edit AI digital avatars. Scene Selection provides rich background templates and customization options. Project Management helps organize and manage video content, supporting folder classification and tag management.

## Creating Your First Video

### Choosing a Digital Avatar

After clicking "Create Video," you first need to choose a digital avatar. You can select from the preset avatar library, featuring over 500 public digital avatars covering different genders, ages, skin tones, and styles. If you need a personalized avatar, click "Custom Avatar" to upload your photo, and the system will generate an exclusive digital avatar based on the photo.

When choosing an avatar, you can preview example videos of different angles and expressions to ensure you select the avatar most suitable for your content needs. After selection, that avatar will become the main character of the current video.

### Entering Video Content

Enter the content you want the digital avatar to narrate in the script editing area. You can directly enter text or upload a pre-prepared audio file. When entering text, the system displays the estimated video duration in real time. Professional terms, names, and place names can be manually adjusted using the platform's pronunciation correction function to ensure accurate AI voiceover.

HeyGen supports multiple input methods: pure text script, audio files (MP3, WAV), and PPT or PDF document import. For long-form content, the system automatically segments it into paragraphs suitable for video presentation.

### Selecting Voice and Language

Choose voice options from the right panel. HeyGen provides over 1,000 AI voices covering more than 175 languages and dialects. You can preview each voice's example to select the tone best matching your content's style. Voice parameters including speed, pitch, and pause intervals can all be fine-tuned as needed.

Multilingual support is one of HeyGen's core advantages. If you need to create multilingual versions, simply select the target language in settings, and the system will automatically match the corresponding voice and perform translation. The translation feature preserves the original speaker's voice characteristics and expression style.

### Adding Backgrounds and Scenes

Choosing an appropriate background can significantly enhance video professionalism. HeyGen provides over 75 preset scenes covering various styles including offices, studios, cityscapes, and natural environments. You can also upload custom background images as video backgrounds.

Dynamic elements like text titles, icon animations, and data charts can enhance information delivery. All elements support personalized adjustments including position, size, color, and animation effects.

### Generating and Exporting Video

After completing all settings, click "Generate" to submit the video creation task. Video generation time depends on video length, complexity, and server load, generally completing within a few minutes. You can view progress in the task list.

After video generation completes, you can preview the final effect. If adjustments are needed, return to the editing interface to modify the script or settings. When satisfied, choose the appropriate format and resolution for export. HeyGen supports multiple resolutions including 720p, 1080p, and 4K, as well as common video formats like MP4 and MOV.

## Advanced Features

### Custom Digital Avatar

Click the "Avatar" option in the navigation bar to enter the avatar management page. Click "Create Custom Avatar" to upload a portrait photo - using a front-facing high-resolution photo is recommended for best results. The system automatically analyzes photo features to generate the corresponding digital avatar.

Custom avatars support rich parameter adjustments including hairstyle, facial features, clothing style, and accessories. You can save multiple avatar settings for quick switching in different videos. Enterprise users can customize exclusive brand avatars for corporate promotion and customer service scenarios.

### Team Collaboration

Business plan users can create team accounts and invite team members to join. Administrators can set usage permissions for different members, distributing video quotas and management functions. Assets created by team members can be set to shared or private, facilitating knowledge accumulation and reuse.

Business accounts can also customize brand elements including logos, exclusive colors, and intro/outro segments. All videos created with the business brand will automatically add brand identifiers, ensuring brand consistency.

### API Integration

For enterprise users needing to integrate HeyGen into existing workflows, the platform provides complete API interfaces. The API supports pay-as-you-go models including Video Agent, Video Translation, and Text-to-Speech functions.

Through API, you can automate video creation processes for large-scale content production. Detailed API documentation and integration guides are available in the developer documentation.

## Account Management

### Subscription Plan Management

You can view your current subscription status and available quotas in the account settings page. You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

After canceling your subscription, you can still use subscription features until the end of the current billing cycle. After the billing cycle ends, the account will automatically switch to the free version.

### Security Settings

It is recommended to enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to enhance account security. Enterprise users can configure SAML/SSO for single sign-on to meet enterprise security policy requirements. The account settings also allow managing active sessions and viewing login history.
