# HeyGen Introduction

## What is HeyGen

HeyGen is an AI video generation platform developed by Shanghai Zhi Xiang Future Technology Co., Ltd., representing the cutting edge of artificial intelligence in video content creation. The platform can quickly convert text, images, or audio into high-quality professional videos, featuring AI digital avatars, voice synthesis, translation, and style matching functions. Since its launch, HeyGen has helped global users generate over 109 million videos, create over 84 million digital avatars, and complete over 15 million video translation tasks. These impressive numbers prove HeyGen's leading position and widespread user recognition in the AI video generation field.

HeyGen's core technology is based on advanced generative AI models, including industry-leading models like Sora, Veo, and Kling for video generation, Flux for high-fidelity image generation, and ElevenLabs for ultra-realistic voice synthesis. The deep integration of these technologies enables HeyGen to generate AI videos that are nearly indistinguishable from real videos. Whether it's the subtle changes in facial expressions, perfect lip-sync with voice, or natural body movements, HeyGen excels in all aspects.

The design philosophy of HeyGen is to make video creation as simple as writing a document. Through its core product AI Studio, users can create training, marketing, sales, and internal content in a unified workspace while enjoying enterprise-level security and management controls. This streamlined process from draft to final video enables anyone to quickly get started and create professional-grade videos without any video editing experience.

## Core Features

### Text to Video

Text to video is one of HeyGen's most core features. Users simply provide script text, and HeyGen's AI video generator automatically handles the entire video creation process, generating high-quality 1080p or 4K cinema-grade videos. This process includes automatically adding voiceover, visual effects, and AI digital avatars, requiring no video production experience from users. It is ideal for creating product introductions, sales promotions, onboarding training, or YouTube content.

This feature supports various combinations of voices, languages, and styles for output. Users can describe the video content they want, and the system generates high-quality videos with minimal manual intervention, making it an ideal choice for content creators and businesses requiring rapid video production.

### Photo to Video

The Photo to Video feature allows users to transform any static image into a dynamic video within seconds. Simply upload an image and add a script, and HeyGen will transform it into an AI-generated video with realistic AI lip-sync, natural voice timing, and expressive voice. This feature can further optimize video effects, including adding text overlays, background music, and smooth transitions, without manual editing.

HeyGen's Photo to Video supports over 175 languages and dialects with various AI voice and accent options. This makes creating multilingual video content easier than ever, whether for local markets or global audiences.

### Product Ads

HeyGen's Product Ads feature revolutionizes e-commerce and brand marketing content creation. Users only need to upload product images and scripts, and the AI ad generator automatically handles the rest, including placing the product in the digital avatar's hands with music, subtitles, and voiceover support. This feature is perfect for creating TikTok, Instagram, and Amazon content at scale without shipping samples or hiring production crews.

This feature is especially suitable for e-commerce sellers, social media marketers, and brands who can quickly generate large volumes of video content with consistent brand imagery. The automated process significantly reduces video ad production costs and time cycles.

### UGC Ads

User-Generated Content (UGC) Ads feature allows users to create viral social media content without cameras or studios. From product reviews and news updates to story-style videos where digital avatars react to trending clips, HeyGen helps create and scale "faceless" channels and personal brands on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube Shorts.

This creative approach is especially suitable for social media creators and influencers who want to maintain anonymity while consistently producing high-quality video content. AI-driven editing tools enable anyone to quickly create eye-catching social content.

### AI Avatar Generator

HeyGen's AI Avatar Generator is one of the platform's core technologies. Users can create realistic digital avatars from photos, videos, or custom prompts. The platform provides a rich library of preset digital avatars, and users can also create their own custom avatars with customizable expressions, gestures, clothing, backgrounds, and movements, including realistic face-swapping AI functions.

The three main avatar types include: Photo Avatar (transforms any static portrait into dynamic video), Public Avatar (diverse high-quality preset images without filming), and Digital Twin (creates a digital clone extremely similar to the user, completely replicating appearance, movements, and voice for unlimited expansion of personal branding).

### AI Translator

HeyGen's AI video translation feature supports over 175 languages and dialects for one-click multilingual content creation. After users upload a video, HeyGen's AI translator translates it into the target language while maintaining natural lip-sync and subtitles, preserving the original speaker's voice, tone, and rhythm. This means there's no need to re-record, hire voice actors, or manually sync audio.

This feature supports culturally accurate translations, maintaining the original speaker's tone, expression style, and personality. Users can automatically translate videos via API to localize AI videos at scale and speed for global audiences. Voice cloning and AI lip-sync ensure users' voices remain consistent across various languages.

### AI Studio

AI Studio is HeyGen's text-based video editor, simplifying the video creation experience to be as easy as writing a document. Creators can complete all work from draft to final video in a clean interface, ensuring no compromises between speed, quality, and authenticity. The user-friendly design of AI Studio enables anyone to create professional-grade video content.

According to user feedback, HeyGen has helped achieve a 10x increase in video production speed and a 5x growth in video creation volume. Many businesses have integrated HeyGen into their content creation workflows, significantly improving marketing efficiency and training content output quality.
