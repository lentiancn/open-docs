# LivePortrait Introduction

LivePortrait is an open-source portrait animation technology developed by Kuaishou Technology in collaboration with the University of Science and Technology of China and Fudan University. Released in July 2024, this project aims to transform static portrait images into dynamic videos, enabling the subjects in photos to perform based on the expressions and head movements from driving videos.

## Technical Background

Portrait animation technology represents an important research direction in computer vision and generative artificial intelligence. Traditional portrait animation methods typically require substantial computational resources and struggle to achieve precise control over generated results. LivePortrait addresses these challenges through innovative stitching and retargeting control mechanisms, delivering efficient and high-quality portrait animation generation.

## Core Capabilities

LivePortrait offers several core technical capabilities:

**Static Image Driving**: Users simply provide a static portrait image and a driving video; the system generates realistic dynamic portraits. The expressions, eye movements, and head poses from the driving video are seamlessly transferred to the subject in the static image.

**Multi-Subject Support**: Beyond human portrait animation, LivePortrait includes animal models capable of animating portraits of cats and dogs. This extends the technology's application to pet content creation and related fields.

**Video Editing**: LivePortrait supports portrait video editing (Video-to-Video) functionality, allowing users to modify expressions and head movements in existing videos for creative content reimagining.

**Precise Control**: Through stitching and retargeting control mechanisms, users can fine-tune facial details in the generated results, including expression intensity and head rotation range.

## Application Scenarios

Typical applications of LivePortrait include social media content creation, bringing static photos to life with dynamic speech; film and television post-production for rapid virtual character animation generation; virtual anchors and digital human driving; creative advertising and short video production, and more.

## Development History

Since its release in July 2024, LivePortrait has undergone multiple significant updates. August 2024 brought image driving mode and regional control support, followed by precise portrait editing capabilities. Windows one-click installer was released in August 2024, and security vulnerability fixes for transformers and gradio libraries came in October 2024. The animal model received an update with additional training data in January 2025.

## Platform Support

LivePortrait supports multiple operating systems and hardware platforms, including Linux, Windows, and macOS with Apple Silicon. The project offers Python inference scripts, Gradio web interface, and HuggingFace online demo spaces to meet diverse user needs.

## Community Ecosystem

Following its open-source release, LivePortrait has gained widespread attention and been integrated by major video platforms and creative tools. Creative content based on this technology appears on platforms including Kuaishou, Douyin, Jianying, and WeChat Channels. The project community has also contributed numerous derivative tools, including real-time driving versions, ComfyUI nodes, WebUI extensions, and various other implementations.

## Technical Specifications

LivePortrait is implemented using the PyTorch framework and provides pretrained model weights for download. The project requires Python 3.10 environment with CUDA acceleration support. For inference speed, real-time or near-real-time processing can be achieved on RTX 4090 graphics cards.
