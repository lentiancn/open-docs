# FantasyTalking Introduction

FantasyTalking is a technology project focused on speech synthesis and dialogue systems.

## What is FantasyTalking

Provides natural and smooth voice interaction experience for smart customer service, audio books, voice assistants and more.

## Main Features

- Speech synthesis
- Dialogue management
- Multi-platform support
- Customizability

## Applicable Scenarios

- Smart customer service
- Audio book generation
- Voice assistant
- Educational applications
- Game NPC dialogue
