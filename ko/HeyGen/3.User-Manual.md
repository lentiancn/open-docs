# HeyGen User Manual

## Creating Video

1. Click "Create Video"
2. Choose digital human avatar
3. Enter script text
4. Select voice and background
5. Generate and download video

## Digital Human Management

- Create custom digital humans
- Upload photos to generate digital humans
- Edit digital human appearance

## Team Collaboration

- Create workspace
- Add team members
- Share projects

## Best Practices

1. Use clear scripts
2. Choose appropriate background music
3. Preview before exporting
4. Use templates for quick creation
