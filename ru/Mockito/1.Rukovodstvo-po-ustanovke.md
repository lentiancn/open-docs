# Rukovodstvo po ustanovke Mockito

Rukovodstvo po ustanovke Mockito v osnovnykh instrumentakh sborki Java.

---

## Maven

```xml
<dependency>
    <groupId>org.mockito</groupId>
    <artifactId>mockito-core</artifactId>
    <version>5.8.0</version>
    <scope>test</scope>
</dependency>
```

---

## Gradle

```groovy
testImplementation 'org.mockito:mockito-core:5.8.0'
```
