# HeyGen

AI Digital Avatar Video Generation Platform

## Introduction

HeyGen is an innovative AI video generation platform that creates realistic digital avatar videos using advanced artificial intelligence technology. Users can quickly generate AI-narrated video content with natural expressions and lip-sync by entering text, uploading images, or audio.

## Main Features

- **Text to Video**: Convert text scripts into professional videos
- **Photo to Video**: Transform static images into dynamic videos
- **AI Digital Avatar**: Create realistic virtual characters
- **AI Translation**: Support for 175+ languages
- **AI Studio**: Unified video creation workspace
- **Multi-platform Export**: 720p, 1080p, 4K

## Quick Start

1. Visit HeyGen official website and register an account
2. Choose a digital avatar or create a custom avatar
3. Enter video script or upload audio
4. Select voice and background
5. Generate and download video
