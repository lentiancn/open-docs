# HeyGen FAQ

## Basic Questions

### What is HeyGen

HeyGen is an AI video generation platform focused on creating realistic digital avatar videos. Users can quickly generate AI-narrated video content by entering text, uploading images or audio. The platform uses advanced deep learning technology to generate digital avatar videos with natural expressions and lip-sync, suitable for marketing videos, enterprise training, online education, content creation, and other scenarios. Without video production experience, users can create professional-grade video works.

HeyGen's core technology is based on generative AI models including Sora, Veo, Kling for video generation, Flux for image generation, and ElevenLabs for voice synthesis. The platform supports over 175 languages and dialects, making it one of the world's leading AI video generation tools.

### Is HeyGen Free

HeyGen offers both free and paid versions. The free version allows 3 videos per month, up to 3 minutes each, suitable for experiencing the platform's basic features. Paid versions include Creator ($29/month), Pro ($99/month), and Business ($149/month), unlocking all advanced features and higher usage quotas.

Free trial requires no credit card; you can start experiencing immediately after registration. Paid versions offer unlimited video creation, higher video duration limits, custom digital avatars, team collaboration features, and other value-added services.

### What Languages are Supported

HeyGen supports over 175 languages and dialects including Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Russian, and more. Voice cloning functionality also supports multilingual voice training, meeting international content creation needs.

The multilingual translation feature preserves the original speaker's voice, tone, and expression style, enabling natural multilingual video content.

### What are the Main Features

HeyGen's core features include: Text to Video, Photo to Video, Product Ads, UGC Ads, AI Avatar Generator (Photo Avatar, Public Avatar, Digital Twin), AI Video Translation, AI Studio Editor, and API Integration.

Users can also access the latest generative AI models, including Sora, Veo, Kling for video generation, Flux for image generation, and ElevenLabs for voice synthesis.

## Technical Questions

### How is the Video Quality

Video quality generated by HeyGen depends on selected parameters. The platform supports multiple resolutions from 720p to 4K. In HD mode, digital avatar facial details, expression changes, and lip-sync are very realistic, making it difficult for average users to distinguish AI-generated videos from real video recordings.

4K resolution is suitable for large-screen display or professional occasions. 1080p is the best choice for social media platforms, balancing clarity and loading speed.

### How Fast is Generation Speed

Video generation speed depends on multiple factors including video length, server load, and selected effect complexity. Generally, 1-minute videos can be generated within a few minutes. Peak hours may require longer wait times.

Paid versions (Creator, Pro) provide faster video processing. Free versions use standard processing speed. It is recommended to plan video production time in advance to avoid rushing.

### How is Data Security Ensured

HeyGen prioritizes user data security. All uploaded photos and audio are stored after encryption processing. The platform has passed multiple international security certifications including GDPR, SOC 2 Type II, CCPA, AI ACT, and DPF, meeting international data protection standards.

Enterprise users can apply for private deployment solutions to achieve complete data self-control. The platform also provides enterprise-grade security features including fine-grained permission management, usage quota controls, and audit logs.

### Can Videos be Used for Commercial Purposes

Videos generated with paid versions can be used for commercial purposes. Enterprise users can use videos for product promotion, brand marketing, customer service, and other commercial scenarios. It should be noted that HeyGen should not be used to generate false news, misleading content, or content that infringes on others' rights. For detailed usage terms, please refer to the service agreement.

## Usage Questions

### How to Create a Custom Digital Avatar

Steps to create a custom digital avatar: Go to the "Avatar" management page; click "Create Custom Avatar"; upload a portrait photo (front-facing high-resolution photo recommended); wait for the system to analyze and generate the digital avatar; adjust facial feature parameters as needed; save the custom avatar for future use.

Custom avatars can be used multiple times, selected directly when creating videos each time. The platform also continuously optimizes custom avatar generation effects. The Digital Twin feature requires uploading a video to create a digital clone extremely similar to the person.

### Can Multiple Videos be Created Simultaneously

Yes, HeyGen supports batch video creation. You can create multiple tasks, and the system will process them in sequence. Paid versions support higher concurrent processing capabilities.

Enterprise users with large video production needs can contact customer service for customized solutions.

### What Video Formats are Supported for Export

HeyGen supports multiple video format exports including MP4 (most commonly used, best compatibility), MOV (suitable for Apple ecosystem), and AVI. Resolutions support 720p, 1080p, and 4K.

During export, you can choose whether to add subtitles, which can be embedded or as separate SRT files. Pro versions also support watermark removal.

### How to Improve Video Quality

Ways to improve video quality: Select HD or 4K resolution for output; use clear portrait photos as custom avatars; select professional scenes matching content; reasonably use text and graphic elements to enhance information delivery; pay attention to script quality and professionalism.

HeyGen continuously optimizes algorithms to improve generation quality. It is recommended to keep the client updated to receive the latest feature optimizations and performance improvements.

## Account Questions

### What to Do If Password is Forgotten

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the prompts to enter your registration email. The system will send a password reset link to your email; click the link to set a new password. If unable to recover, it is recommended to contact customer service for assistance.

### How to Upgrade Subscription Plan

After logging in, go to account settings and select "Subscription Management" to view current subscription status and available upgrade options. Click the plan you want and follow the prompts to complete payment. Upgrades take effect immediately, and used quotas will be deducted from the new plan.

Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

### Is Refund Available

HeyGen offers free trials; it is recommended to experience the free version or short-term plans before purchasing. After formal subscription, contact customer service to negotiate handling if major service issues occur. For specific refund policies, please refer to the service terms.

### How to Cancel Subscription

You can go directly to your account page to find options to pause or cancel your subscription. After canceling, you can still use subscription features until the end of the current billing cycle. After the billing cycle ends, the account will automatically switch to the free version.

## Copyright and Compliance

### Who Owns Generated Content Copyright

Videos created by users using HeyGen are owned by the users. Users can freely use, publish, and commercially utilize these videos. However, they must comply with relevant content compliance regulations in the service agreement and must not generate illegal or non-compliant content.

### Platform Asset Usage Restrictions

Preset digital avatar images, scene backgrounds, and audio materials provided by HeyGen can only be used within the platform and cannot be downloaded or extracted for other purposes. Business users who need broader usage authorization can contact the business team for customized solutions.

### Handling of Non-Compliant Content

HeyGen has established a complete content review mechanism. The system automatically detects and blocks non-compliant content creation requests. For published non-compliant videos, the platform has the right to remove them and freeze related accounts. Severe violations may result in permanent account termination and legal liability.

Prohibited content includes: False news and misleading information; Violent and hateful content; Pornographic and vulgar content; Content infringing on others' copyrights; Any illegal use of content.
