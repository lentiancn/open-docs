# Rukovodstvo po ispolzovaniyu Mockito

Rukovodstvo po effektivnomu ispolzovaniyu Mockito.

---

## Osnovy

Mockito yavlyaetsya frameworkom dlya mockirovaniya v unit-testakh na Java.

---

## Sozdanie Mock

```java
List<String> mockList = Mockito.mock(List.class);
```

---

## Stubbing

```java
when(mockList.get(0)).thenReturn("first");
```

---

## Verifikaciya

```java
verify(mockList).add("element");
```

---

## Annotacii

```java
@Mock
private UserService userService;
```
