# OpenClaw - Your Personal AI Assistant

OpenClaw is a personal AI assistant that runs on your own devices. It responds on the channels you already use, including WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, Google Chat, Signal, iMessage, IRC, Microsoft Teams, Matrix, Feishu, LINE, Mattermost, Nextcloud Talk, Nostr, Synology Chat, Tlon, Twitch, Zalo, and WebChat. It can speak and listen on macOS/iOS/Android and can render a live Canvas that you control.

## What is OpenClaw

OpenClaw is more than an AI chatbot — it's your personal digital assistant. Unlike traditional AI, OpenClaw can:

- **Get actual work done**: Clear your inbox, send emails, manage your calendar, check you in for flights
- **Run 24/7**: Available whenever you need it
- **Remember persistently**: Keeps track of your preferences and context, continuously learning about you
- **Be proactive**: Works in the background through cron jobs and reminders
- **Stay under your control**: Your data and configuration stay local, not tied to any cloud provider

## Core Features

### Multi-Channel Access

OpenClaw supports over 20 communication platforms. You can chat with it in any messaging app you already use, without switching to something new.

### Local-First Architecture

Your context and skills live on your own computer, not in the cloud. OpenClaw is open source, and you have complete control over your data.

### Powerful Automation

OpenClaw can automate all kinds of tasks, from managing emails to controlling smart devices, from processing documents to scheduling — almost anything you can do on your computer.

### Voice Interaction

Supports voice wake and talk mode. Activate it with wake words on macOS/iOS, or have continuous voice conversations on Android.

### Skills Platform

Through the Skills platform, you can install community-built skills or create your own automation workflows. OpenClaw will automatically search for and pull in new skills as needed.

## Use Cases

- **Personal assistant**: Manage schedules, reminders, process emails
- **Office automation**: Document processing, data organization, meeting preparation
- **Learning assistant**: Material collection, note organization, knowledge management
- **Home management**: Smart home control, family schedule coordination

## Technical Architecture

OpenClaw consists of several core components:

- **Gateway**: The main control service running locally, managing sessions, channels, tools, and events
- **Agent**: Agent runtime running on Raspberry Pi or other devices
- **CLI**: Command-line tool for interacting with OpenClaw
- **Companion Apps**: macOS menu bar app, iOS/Android companion apps
- **Browser Control**: Dedicated browser controlled via CDP
- **Canvas**: Visual workspace with A2UI support

## Why Choose OpenClaw

Unlike commercial AI assistants, OpenClaw is completely open source. You can view, modify, and extend its functionality. It runs on your own devices, and your data is never sent to third-party servers. This open and local-first design philosophy makes OpenClaw truly your own AI assistant.
