# HeyGen FAQ

> Frequently Asked Questions about HeyGen

---

## General

### What is HeyGen?

HeyGen is an AI-powered video generation platform that creates videos using virtual avatars and text-to-speech technology.

### How does it work?

1. Choose or create an avatar
2. Enter your script
3. Select voice and language
4. Generate video

---

## Usage

### How to create a video?

1. Log in to HeyGen dashboard
2. Select "Create Video"
3. Choose a template or avatar
4. Enter your script
5. Customize visuals
6. Generate and download

### What languages are supported?

HeyGen supports 100+ languages including English, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean.

### What video formats are supported?
